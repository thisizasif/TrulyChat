How do I join a channel?

Enter your name, type a channel number, and select Join Channel.

Do I need an account?

No. TrulyChat works without sign-up. Just pick a name and a channel.

How do I invite others?

Share the channel number or use the Invite option from the chat menu.

Can I change my name?

Yes. Use Change name from the menu to update it for your current session.
